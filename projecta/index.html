Nova

Scientific Name:

Novavore Stellaris

Discovered by / at:

Jiho Choi, 27-08-2014

Family:

Nova forms independently, and only one can exist in a single space at any time. Objects absorbed by Nova and later released as stars can potentially transform into a new Nova, but only if left undisturbed for 150 years. Because of this specific and lengthy reproduction process, sightings of Nova are extremely rare.

Habitat:

Nova creatures spawn in empty spaces, such as blank rooms or vacuums, though their appearance is never guaranteed. This emptiness is essential for Nova’s manifestation, as their nature depends on absorbing objects and re-creating them as stars, essentially forming their own galaxy. Without this blank canvas, Nova cannot fulfill its purpose of cosmic creation.

Appearance:

Nova appears as abnormal, pulsing rings of black circles, moving erratically as if lost and searching for something. Once it evolves by absorbing objects, it spreads its influence by darkening the space it occupies and filling it with newly produced stars. During this "star production stage," Nova expands, filling the space with its body, mimicking the orbit of a galaxy, like its own version of the Milky Way. When all its stars are eventually destroyed, Nova shrinks back to its original size.

Key Characteristics:

  • Sucks in objects but also lerps to absorb them
  • Produces colourful stars from the absorbed objects
  • Moves unpredictably and erratically
  • It evolves by completely influencing the appearance of its environment
  • Spawn conditions are tricky

What we know so far about Novavore Stellaris? The Novavore Stellaris is highly unstable in its first form, largely due to the difficulty of meeting and maintaining its specific spawning conditions, which are constantly vulnerable to external influences. It moves erratically, searching desperately for objects to absorb, driven by its desire to create its own family of stars and reproduce. This creature is inherently fragile, and the stars it produces from absorbed objects are even more delicate—capable of being destroyed by the slightest touch or trigger. Novavore Stellaris has a lifespan of around 1,000 years, but even reaching this age is challenging due to the precarious nature of its spawning, reproduction, and incubation process. As a result, these creatures are exceedingly rare, and their population is steadily declining. Their frantic movements reflect this struggle for survival, making each remaining Nova a desperate, endangered presence in the cosmos.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature Click on the empty spaces around Nova to provide it with objects. Once Nova evolves into its second phase, press the "S" key to make it expel the newly created stars. Clicking on the stars will cause them to instantly die in a burst of circles.
